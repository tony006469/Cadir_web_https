About Cadir

We aim to collaborate with exceptional MedTech startups worldwide. When startup founders are committed to leveraging additional capital for rapid business growth, Cadir can become a steadfast partner on this journey for the long haul.

Core Investment: We mainly invest in MedTech business, and we prefer startups the utilize new technologies to improve clinical process more effectively. Our investment size from USD $ 200K to $ 1M. We emphasize long-term relationship with founders to grow the business and achieve founders’ goals together.

Innovating the Future: We believe technology can reshape healthcare's future and should not just primarily focus on maximizing financial returns. We back startups focused on education or rural issues, balancing impact with financial sustainability. Our initial investments range from $100K to $200K, supporting new founders to get them off the ground quickly.

We also facilitate direct connections between startups and resources from IRCAD, Show Chwan Healthcare System, and other shareholders, speeding up business growth.
